About This System

🧠 Model Architecture

Our system uses MobileNetV2, a lightweight yet powerful neural network trained through transfer learning on the HAM10000 skin cancer dataset. This approach ensures both accuracy and efficiency.

📊 Dataset

Trained on HAM10000 (Human Against Machine), a dataset of 10,000 dermatoscopic images. This diverse dataset helps the model handle various skin types and lesion characteristics.

⚠️ Disclaimer

This tool is for informational purposes only and should NOT replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult a dermatologist for accurate diagnosis and treatment.

🔒 Privacy

Your uploaded images are processed locally and not stored on our servers. Your privacy is our priority. No data is retained after analysis.

Technical Specifications

Model: MobileNetV2 Transfer Learning
Input Size: 224 × 224 pixels (RGB)
Classes: Benign, Malignant
Framework: TensorFlow/Keras
Backend: Flask REST API
